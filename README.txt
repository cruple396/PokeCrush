Title:
PokeCrush

Description:
PokeCrush is a Pokemon themed Candy Crush game.

How to execute program:
Open Game.html with a browser. No installing or compiling needed.

How to play:
To play, click start game. You will be taken to a 9x9 board with pokemon images inside.
Drag and drop a selected pokemon to make three, four, or five in a row. 
After 20 turns, the game will end and you will see your score and the high score.
If you beat the high score, you progress to the next level and the high score increases by 100.
If you don't beat the high score, you get sent back to the first level with a high score of 750;
